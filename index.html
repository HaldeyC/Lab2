Biography

My name in Cay Haldey. I am almost 50 years old. I'm not new to computers but I'm pretty new to web development. I have a special memory I like to share. When i was around 8-10 years old I got my first computer. Still remember the model, a Commandore VIC-20. It was not much of a computer if you compair to todays PC's but you could play games on them. The first game I play was "City Bomber". You started the game by pushing in a cartridge in the VIC-20 and flipped the power switch. The game started and a little plane flew from left to right. You pressed the spacebar to drop a bombs on the buildings so the buildings got destroyed. Once all the buildings were destroyed you could land the plane and win the game. This was a very long time ago, but it is still a good memory to me.

I started to try coding again, and this time some stuff really made sense. Applied to differant schools to get some better knowledge. Luckily I got in to Kristianstad University. So now I am learning and evolving in this field.
